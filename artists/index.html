Todor H.

As a dedicated audiophile, Todor H has spent years refining his sound through a host of musical projects. He has been a founding force for the Brussels techno scene, hosting underground parties and developing elaborate live sets. Slowly Todor H is becoming a veteran, a must-know name in the landscape.

Egocent

Egocent is stripped down, straight forward techno, evolving into sometimes deep, experimental forms. It's all about power, momentum, and explosions. Formerly a member of Cybertech, Egocent has followed his musical path from livesets to own production, from playing others' tunes to banging them out himself. Moving between Germany and Belgium, the flavor of the continent penetrates all his work.

Khan

Having years of experience as part of the DJ-duo Midnight Noodles, Khan has stood in some of the biggest venues of Belgium, alongside a great range of international heavy-hitters. He was a resident DJ for Break the Silence collective, co-founder of LUKAS, and has been slowly developing his own productions, looking to contribute to the scene.
